§ 02 · Protocol flow

How it Works

5 steps
Step I

Connect

Connect your wallet to Robinhood Chain (Arbitrum Orbit L2). Get test tokens from the official faucet.

Step II

Get Stocks

Claim tokenized stocks from the RH Chain faucet or provide liquidity to the AtlasVault.

Step III

Create Index

Bundle stocks. Set weights and fees. Smart contract deploys your index token.

Step IV

Others Invest

Investors acquire your index in one transaction. The AtlasVault mints tokens backed 1:1 by real tokenized stocks.

Step V

Earn Fees

Fees split 50/50: creators and LPs earn on every buy/sell. Fully on-chain, transparent distribution.
